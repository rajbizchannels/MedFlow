# MedFlow Executive Demo - Voiceover Script

**Duration:** 13 minutes 30 seconds
**Word Count:** ~1,890 words
**Speaking Rate:** 140 words per minute (moderate, professional pace)
**Tone:** Professional, confident, enthusiastic but not overly sales-y

---

## Recording Instructions

**Before Recording:**
- Find a quiet room with minimal echo
- Use a quality microphone (Blue Yeti, Audio-Technica AT2020, or similar)
- Position mic 6-8 inches from your mouth
- Use a pop filter
- Wear headphones to monitor audio quality
- Do a 30-second test recording and playback

**During Recording:**
- Speak clearly and naturally
- Maintain consistent energy throughout
- Pause briefly at marked pause points [PAUSE]
- Re-record sections if you stumble (don't start over entirely)
- Stay hydrated (keep water nearby)

**Delivery Style:**
- Warm and engaging (like talking to a colleague, not reading)
- Emphasize key phrases (marked in **bold**)
- Slow down slightly for metrics and numbers
- Smile while speaking (it comes through in your voice)

- [ ] Mark personal notes on script (where to emphasize, breathe)
- [ ] Do vocal warm-ups (humming, lip trills)
- [ ] Drink room-temperature water (avoid ice cold)

- [ ] Sit or stand in a comfortable position
- [ ] Keep good posture (affects breathing and voice)
- [ ] Smile while speaking (it changes your tone positively)
- [ ] Take breaks every 5-7 minutes
- [ ] Stay hydrated
- [ ] If you stumble, pause 3 seconds, then re-read the sentence

**Post-Recording:**
- [ ] Listen to the full recording
- [ ] Note any sections that need re-recording
- [ ] Re-record problem sections
- [ ] Export as high-quality WAV (for editing) and MP3 (for backup)

## Audio Editing Checklist

After recording, edit the audio:

- [ ] Remove long pauses (keep 0.5-1s pauses for natural flow)
- [ ] Remove mouth clicks and breaths (but keep some for naturalness)
- [ ] Apply noise reduction (remove background hum)
- [ ] Normalize volume (consistent loudness)
- [ ] Add compression (smooth volume fluctuations)
- [ ] De-ess (reduce harsh "S" sounds)
- [ ] EQ to boost clarity (boost 2-5kHz range slightly)
- [ ] Final limiter (prevent clipping)
- [ ] Export as WAV (48kHz, 24-bit) for video editing

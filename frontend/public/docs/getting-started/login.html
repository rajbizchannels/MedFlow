Login & Authentication

AureonCare uses secure, role-based authentication to ensure patient data privacy and HIPAA compliance. This guide will help you access the system and manage your account.

Accessing AureonCare

Step 1: Navigate to Login Page

  1. Open your web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge recommended)
  2. Navigate to your AureonCare URL (provided by your administrator)
  3. You will see the AureonCare login screen

Step 2: Enter Your Credentials

  1. Username: Enter your assigned username (usually your email address)
  2. Password: Enter your secure password
  3. Click the "Sign In" button
üí° Tip:

Check the "Remember Me" option if you're using a personal, secure device. Do NOT use this option on shared computers.

First-Time Login

When you log in for the first time, you may be asked to:

  • Change Your Password: Create a strong password that meets security requirements
  • Set Up Two-Factor Authentication (2FA): Add an extra layer of security (if enabled)
  • Review Terms of Service: Accept the user agreement and privacy policy
  • Complete Your Profile: Add contact information and preferences

Password Requirements

  • Minimum 8 characters
  • At least one uppercase letter
  • At least one lowercase letter
  • At least one number
  • At least one special character (@, #, $, %, etc.)

Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

If your organization has enabled 2FA, you'll need to verify your identity using a second method after entering your password.

Setting Up 2FA

  1. After entering your password, you'll be prompted to set up 2FA
  2. Choose your preferred method:
    • SMS Text Message: Receive a code via text
    • Authenticator App: Use Google Authenticator or similar app
    • Email: Receive a code via email
  3. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete setup
  4. Save your backup codes in a secure location

User Roles and Permissions

AureonCare uses role-based access control (RBAC). Your role determines which features you can access:

Role Description Key Permissions
Administrator Full system access Manage users, configure settings, view all data
Doctor Clinical provider Patient care, prescribe, order labs, clinical notes
Nurse Clinical support Vital signs, assist with care, view patient data
Receptionist Front desk Schedule appointments, register patients, check-in
Billing Manager Revenue cycle Submit claims, process payments, financial reports
Patient Self-service portal View records, schedule appointments, pay bills

Troubleshooting Login Issues

Forgot Password

  1. Click the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page
  2. Enter your username or email address
  3. Check your email for a password reset link
  4. Click the link and create a new password
  5. Return to the login page and sign in with your new password

Account Locked

If you enter an incorrect password multiple times (usually 5 attempts), your account will be temporarily locked for security. Wait 15 minutes or contact your administrator to unlock it.

Can't Receive 2FA Code

  • Check your phone signal or email inbox
  • Request a new code by clicking "Resend Code"
  • Use a backup code if available
  • Contact your administrator for assistance
üîí Security Best Practices:
  • Never share your password with anyone
  • Log out when leaving your workstation
  • Use a unique password for AureonCare
  • Report suspicious activity immediately
  • Keep your contact information up to date

Logging Out

To securely log out of AureonCare:

  1. Click your profile icon in the top-right corner
  2. Select "Logout" from the dropdown menu
  3. You will be returned to the login screen

Your session will automatically expire after 30 minutes of inactivity for security purposes.
